Alexander Castle

Alexander Castle is one of the most amazing and renowned structure in Mymensingh District. It was built by Maharaja Suryo Kanta Acharya Chowdhury in 1879 in the memory of the 1st zilla magistrate of Mymensingh Mr. N .S Alexander at the time of Jubili festival. It covers an area of 27.155 acres.

Alexander Castle
Address: Mymensingh, Bangladesh
Opening hours: 9am - 5pm (daily)

Bepin Park

Choto Shona Mosque Small Golden Mosque) is located in Chapai Nawabganj district of Bangladesh. The mosque is situated about 3 kilometres (1.9 mi) south of the Kotwali Gate and 0.5 kilometres (0.31 mi) to the south-east of the Mughal Tahakhana complex in the Firozpur Quarter.

Bepin Park
Address: Mymensingh, Bangladesh
Website: Wikipedia
Opening hours: Mon - Sat: 10am - 5pm (closed on Sun)
