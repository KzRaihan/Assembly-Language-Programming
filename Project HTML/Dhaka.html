Ahsan Manzil Museum

Highlights of Ahsan Manzil Museum Ahsan Manzil Pink Palace is a completely pink building, which shows the life of the earth emperors called Raj at that time through real objects and photos. Ahsan Manzil Pink Palace (Ahsan Manzil) is a private residence rebuilt in 1872 by a landlord, Nawab Abdul Ghani, on the basis of a barn left by the French. The mansion was damaged by a tornado and has been rebuilt to look even more spectacular.
